🚀 The Real Power of AI – From 0 to My Own Custom Portfolio Website!

Posted by Suryansh Johri | July 2025

We’re living in a new era where almost anything is possible with the help of AI tools like ChatGPT.

Just a few months ago, I had zero knowledge of JavaScript and only a basic understanding of HTML & CSS.
Yet today, I’m proud to share something I built myself:
👉 My fully customized portfolio website.

Back in March, I had a vision — a website that reflected my personality, not just another template.
But I struggled. I didn’t have the skills to make it real.

Yes, ChatGPT helped me — but I quickly learned:
⚠️ AI doesn’t replace learning — it amplifies it.

So I made a decision:

  • 📚 Learned the basics of web development (HTML + CSS)
  • 💻 Spent countless hours experimenting, failing, and improving
  • 🤝 Used AI smartly — not blindly — to support my creativity

Today, I can proudly say:

  • ✅ This is my website
  • ✅ Written with my code
  • ✅ Designed in my style
💡 Takeaway:
You don’t need to be a pro.
You just need to start.
Pair basic learning with smart AI use, and you’ll be amazed at what you can build.
